Euro 2024 Last Man Balling

Predict the Euro 2024 top performers to win big. The more players, the bigger the prize.

The deadline to join is 5pm BST on Friday 14th June.

How to Play

It's £10 to enter, to be paid by bank transfer. All players must provide a mobile number, to be added to a WhatsApp group and receive payment details.

Pick 2 or 3 teams without exceeding the £50 total budget. Earn 3 points for each win and 1 point for each draw, based on your teams' performance throughout the entirety of Euro 2024. Draws only count in the group stage as the three points will be allocated to the team that progresses through the knockout rounds, regardless of the score at full-time.

Teams cannot be replaced.

If you go over the £50 budget, you will not be included in the game so please double check your total cost before submitting your selections.

The player whose chosen teams have the most points at the end, wins the entire prize pot.

Tiebreaker

In the event of a tie at the end of the tournament, such as if more than one person picks the same teams and gets the same points, the winner will be decided by a tiebreaker. When submitting teams, everyone must predict the total number of goals scored throughout the tournament.

Prizes

The winner will receive ALL the entry money collected. There are no prizes for second or third place. The more people who play, the bigger the prize, so invite your friends to join the fun!
