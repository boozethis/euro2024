FAQ

What is Last Man Balling?

Last Man Balling is a weekly Premier League competition where you choose a team to win each week. If your team wins, you earn 3 points; if they draw, you earn 1 point; and if they lose, you earn 0 points. The Baller with the most points after 20 weeks wins the Prizepot.

How much does it cost to join?

The entry fee is ¬£5.

How do I pay?

All payments are taken via Stripe. When the game reopens for signups, a link to pay will be provided. Stripe accepts all major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

When is the deadline to submit my selection?

The deadline to submit your selection is the kickoff of the first match in the current Gameweek. Gameweeks are the same as those used in Fantasy Premier League.

How do I submit my selection?

You must submit your weekly selection via the WhatsApp group.

Can I see which teams I have selected so far?

Yes, all selections so far will be viewable on a public spreadsheet.

Can I pay ¬£10 to be auto-enrolled in the second game?

No, you must wait until the first game has finished before paying to join the next game, starting in Gameweek 21.

What if I do not submit a selection before the deadline?

If you fail to submit a selection before the deadline, you will automatically be assigned the next alphabetical team that you haven't already used. For example, if you choose Arsenal in Gameweek 1 and fail to submit a selection in Gameweek 2, you will be assigned Aston Villa. Please try to submit your selection on time to avoid unwanted auto-selection.

Can I submit my selections way ahead of the deadline, or even for all 20 Gameweeks?

Yes of course, you are more than welcome to do this. You may use this form if you wish to submit your selections way in advance of the Gameweek deadlines. You are still welcome to edit any selections made using the form, so long as you do so before the relevant gameweek deadline.

What if the team I select has their game postponed?

If you select a team from a game that is postponed, you will not earn any points for that gameweek and you cannot choose that team again.

Can I play if I am outside of the UK?

Yes, anyone over the age of 18 from anywhere in the world can join.

Can I play if I am under 18?

No. Due to the nature of the Last Man Balling rules, gambling activities regulated under the Gambling Act 2005 are restricted to over 18s. Anyone found to be taking part who is under 18 will be removed from the game and will not be refunded.

Unfortunately, you cannot join once the game has started. However, you can sign up for notifications to join the second game, starting in Gameweek 21 in January. Please sign up to the Waiting List to be notified when the next game starts.

Sure, if you're really that desperate to win, you can enter as many times as you like. Each entry will cost you ¬£5.
