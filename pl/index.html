FAQ

What is Last Man Balling?

Last Man Balling is a weekly soccer competition where you choose a team to win each week. If your team wins, you stay in the game; if they lose or draw, you're out.

How much does it cost to join?

The entry fee is ¬£10.

How do I pay?

You can pay via the embedded Stripe payment form in the Join section.

What happens if my team draws?

If your team draws, you are eliminated from the competition.

How is the prizepot calculated?

The prizepot is the total entry fees minus any admin costs.

Can I join after the competition starts?

No, entries close at the deadline before the first game of each week.
