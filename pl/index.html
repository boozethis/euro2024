FAQ

What is Last Man Balling?

Last Man Balling is a weekly soccer competition where you choose a team to win each week. If your team wins, you stay in the game; if they lose or draw, you're out.

How much does it cost to join?

The entry fee is ¬£10.

How do I pay?

You can pay via the embedded Stripe payment form in the Join section.

When is the deadline to submit my selection?

The deadline is approximately 6PM every Friday before the first game of the week.

How do I submit my selection?

You must submit your weekly selection via [this Google Form]. Please include your name, your PIN, and your prediction for the week.

Can I see which teams I have selected so far?

Yes, all selections so far can be viewed on [this Google Sheets document].

What if my team draws?

You only keep your lives intact if your team wins. If your team loses or draws, you will lose a life.

Can I earn or buy more lives?

No, it is not possible to recover lost lives, or earn more than the three you start with.

What if I do not submit a selection before the deadline?

If you fail to submit a selection before the deadline, you will be assigned the first team that you haven‚Äôt already selected, alphabetically.

What if the team I select has their game postponed?

If you select a team from a game that is postponed, you will progress to the next Gameweek without losing a life.

What happens if everyone is eliminated and nobody wins?

If everyone is eliminated without a single Baller remaining, there will be a Ballover. The game will restart, and everyone wishing to play again must pay the ¬£10 entry fee which will be added to the existing Prizepot. All team selections are reset, and the game starts again completely.

If I pick a later kickoff, will I be eliminated later than other Ballers?

No, kickoff times are not taken into account. Whoever loses a life in a Gameweek, loses it all at the same time. You cannot prevent a Ballover by choosing a later kickoff.

Can I play if I am outside of the UK?

Yes, anyone over the age of 18 from anywhere in the world can join.

Can I play if I am under 18?

No. Due to the nature of the Last Man Balling rules, gambling activities regulated under the Gambling Act 2005 are restricted to over 18s.

Can I play if I don‚Äôt have social media?

Yes, but all players are encouraged to join the official Last Man Balling WhatsApp group and follow @lastmanballing on Twitter and Instagram to keep up to date with the latest fixtures, selections, eliminations and more.

I missed the chance to sign up before Round 1. Can I still play?

Unfortunately, you cannot join mid-game. However, in the event of a Ballover where no singular winner is declared, you will be able to join. Please sign up to the Waiting List to be notified in the event of a Ballover. As soon as Round 1 starts, it will not be possible to pay the ¬£10 entry fee to join the game.

Can I join multiple times?

No. All Ballers can only join once, and anyone found to be submitting multiple selections will be eliminated from the game and will not receive a refund.

Can I pay in advance for any potential Ballovers?

No, you can only pay ¬£10 for the current game.
